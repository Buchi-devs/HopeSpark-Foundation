Breaking Barriers

Established in 2014 as an initiative of Spark of Hope Foundation, we began by addressing the immediate needs of single mothers facing economic hardship in local communities.

Building Foundations

By 2018, our programs expanded to provide education, career training, and childcare support for over 10,000 women, creating pathways to financial independence.

Future Vision

Our 2030 goal: empower 1 million women and their families through holistic support systems, advocacy, and sustainable community development.
