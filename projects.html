Sustainable Farming Initiative

Our agricultural program is empowering rural communities to achieve food security and economic independence through sustainable farming techniques.

  • Training: Providing agricultural skills and knowledge to local farmers
  • Resources: Supplying seeds, tools, and irrigation systems
  • Market Access: Creating connections to local and regional markets
