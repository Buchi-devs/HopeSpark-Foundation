Program Overview

Our 12-month incubator program provides women entrepreneurs with the tools, training and funding to launch sustainable businesses in underserved communities. Participants receive:

  • $5,000-$25,000 seed funding
  • Business development training
  • One-on-one mentorship
  • Access to investor networks

Success Story

"The incubator program transformed my small home bakery into a thriving catering business. With the $15,000 seed funding and mentorship, I now employ 8 other women and supply three local schools with daily meals."
- Joan Runner, Founder of Nourish Catering

Program Overview

Our 6-month intensive program equips women with essential digital skills and provides the technology needed to participate in the modern workforce. The program includes:

  • Free laptop or tablet for all participants
  • Coding bootcamp (HTML, CSS, JavaScript)
  • Digital marketing training
  • Job placement assistance

Success Story

"Before this program, I had never touched a computer. Now I work as a junior web developer earning three times what I made as a cleaner. The skills I learned changed my life and my family's future."
- Maria Gutierrez, Junior Developer at TechSolutions

Program Overview

Our comprehensive scholarship program identifies and supports exceptional young women from developing nations to pursue undergraduate and graduate degrees at top universities worldwide. Benefits include:

  • Full tuition coverage and living stipend
  • Leadership development training
  • Career mentorship program
  • Global alumni network

Success Story

"Coming from a rural village where girls rarely finish high school, this scholarship allowed me to study engineering at Stanford. I'm now working on renewable energy solutions to bring electricity to communities like mine."
- Naledi Moshoeshoe, Renewable Energy Engineer
