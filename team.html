Our Team

Meet the celebrities who are changing the world through their extraordinary charitable efforts.

Our Mission

We believe in the power of influence for good. Our team of celebrity ambassadors uses their platform to drive meaningful change in communities around the world.

Keanu Reeves

Children's Hospitals & Cancer Research

$50M+ donated Since 2003

Known for his quiet generosity, Keanu has donated millions to children's hospitals and cancer research, inspired by his sister's battle with leukemia.

Children Education

Dolly Parton

Imagination Library & COVID Research

200M+ books donated Since 1995

Through her Imagination Library, Dolly has donated over 200 million books to children. She also donated $1M to COVID-19 vaccine research.

Education Health

Leonardo DiCaprio

Environmental Conservation

$100M+ donated Since 1998

Through his foundation, Leo has awarded over $100M in grants to environmental organizations working to protect wildlife and restore balance to threatened ecosystems.

Environment Health

Chance the Rapper

Chicago Public Schools

$4.2M+ donated Since 2017

Through his nonprofit SocialWorks, Chance has donated millions to Chicago public schools and mental health initiatives, focusing on youth empowerment through arts and education.

Children Education

Mila Kunis

Ukraine Relief & Children's Hospitals

$37M+ raised Since 2022

Born in Ukraine, Mila raised over $37 million for Ukrainian refugees and has been a longtime supporter of children's hospitals in Los Angeles.

Children Health

Yvon Chouinard

Patagonia Founder & Environmental Activist

$3B+ company donated Since 2022

In an unprecedented move, Chouinard donated his entire $3 billion company to fight climate change, transferring ownership to trusts that will use all profits for environmental causes.
